Nature Protection Areas (Germany)

The data of german nature protection areas are provided by the Bundesamt für Naturschutz (Federal Agency for Nature Conservation) under the GeoNutzV law. Additionally, we have an explicit written permission.
The displayed data sets are merged by the Bundesamt für Naturschutz from data sets of the individual federal states. The Bundesamt für Naturschutz does not provide any warranty regarding the precision and correctness of the data.
On the website of the Bundesamt you can find an official map showing the protection areas.

Geocaching Data

The displayed geocaching data is gathered by the Opencaching-API (OKAPI) from the national Opencaching sites Opencaching.de, Opencaching.pl, Opencaching.nl, Opencaching.org.uk, Opencaching.us, Opencaching.ro and is subject to the associated data licenses.

Cookies

This site uses cookies to store the current map view (center, markers, lines, etc.) in order to be able to restore on a recurring visit. You can, of course, delete these cookies in your browser settings.

Piwik

This website uses Piwik, a web analytics open-source software. Piwik uses “cookies”, which are text files placed on your computer, to help the website analyze how users use the site. The information generated by the cookie about your use of the website will be stored on the server of the service provider in Germany. You may refuse the use of cookies by selecting the appropriate settings on your browser, however please note that if you do this you may not be able to use the full functionality of this website.
